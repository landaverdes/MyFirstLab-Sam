How to build a pc for beginners!!!

Building a pc is a lot of fun. It is a very easy process if you understand how to do it.
I built my first pc two years ago and it was one of the best experiences of my life. Click here to watch a video on how to build your first pc



Components to build your pc

  1. A motherboard
  2. A Central Procesing Unit(CPU)
  3. A Graphics Processing Unit(GPU)
    4. Best known as graphics card
  4. Random Access Memory(RAM)
  5. Storage:SSD or HDD
  6. Power supply unit
  7. A case
